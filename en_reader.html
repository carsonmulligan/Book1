Chapter One:
Dust and Blue Cotton

Wang Fanzhi's Verse

"The robe wears its own silence
Dust settles where the face turns from mirrors
Who dares name this naked truth?
Not I, merely the wind's finger tracing river's spine"

The saddle became a sundial after the third day. Jack Horner watched moonlight pool in its leather concavity...

"Your map's bleeding," the farmer said, tobacco smoke curling into Song dynasty cursive...

At moon zenith, the farmer began writing with root tips. Characters bloomed in the mud: 無住...

The revelation came with frog chorus. Jack watched his reflection in irrigation water multiply...

Commentary

Wang's "dustless face" finds its mirror in the Texan's surrender to mud. Note how agricultural implements become divination tools...
